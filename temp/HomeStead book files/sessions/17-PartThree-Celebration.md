# PART THREE: Celebration

In **Part Three** of this rite of passage, you will share your learning with your peers, family, and church community. In this major movement of the process, you will write a personal testimony to be shared before the church. As you reflect on the past weeks, you will also seriously reflect on your commitment to raise your children in the discipline and instruction of the Lord. Last, you will enjoy a ceremony of celebration that initiates you into parenting duty and family life. The final stage of this process is meant to encourage you, celebrate God's work in your life, mark this significant milestone, and present you before the church community as an active member who takes life and faith seriously, for the glory of God.
