### Tuning Our Hearts to the Lord

*Unite your hearts together in worship. Use the community confession, corporate song, Scripture reading, responsive call, and opening prayer to enter God's presence together.*

#### Community Confession

By God's grace and through faith in Christ, our home belongs to the Lord. As priests, we will nurture our children in God's fatherly care. As stewards, we will pass on the faith of our fathers. As shepherds, we will lead our children as God shepherds us. As teachers, we will instruct our household in the fear of the Lord. As protectors, we will entrust our family to the God's safekeeping. As watchmen, we will build Christian hearth and home as a heritage from the Lord. **As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord. For God's glory, for our good, and for the sake of the world! Amen and amen!**

#### Opening Prayer

Faithful Lord, we dedicate our home to you. Protect us from false gods and idolatry, lest we give our hearts and homes to that which will only disappoint and destroy. Keep us from wrong belief, which will lead us into all forms of godlessness. Instead, help us to be strong and obey all of your word. Prevent us from becoming enamored with worldly delights. Rather, help us to have an eternal vision for our everlasting home. We devote our marriage, our children, our family, and our guests to you. Root out sin and evil. Plant within us truth and holiness. Remove our cowardice and compromise. Establish us in courage and wholehearted devotion. Depose impostor kings and counterfeit gods. On the contrary, build us firm upon your word, pure in the worship of God, and at peace in your all-knowing and all-powerful embrace. Amen.
