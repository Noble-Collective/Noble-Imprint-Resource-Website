In **Part Four** of this rite of passage, you are given blank space for your personal reflection and devotional study. Throughout the weeks of this journey, there will be many things worth recording. You will experience moments and conversations that you will want to remember. You will glean timeless truths and biblical teachings that will serve you for the rest of your life. You will become aware of yourself with renewed insight and specific detail. Since this is only a beginning phase in your journey, you are certain to encounter questions and concerns that you will want to explore further. In support of the very important practice of spiritual journaling, this journal space is meant to encourage you to begin healthy habits of spiritual and devotional reflection.
